``periodic`` is a child of <<CC_Fields_{listname}_request_payment, payment>>. +
``periodic`` references recurring transactions such as payments of a subscription or an installment. +
For all recurring payments the fields <<CC_Fields_{listname}_request_periodic, ``periodic-type``>> and <<CC_Fields_{listname}_request_periodic, ``sequence-type``>> must be provided with a value.
